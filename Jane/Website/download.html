Downloading Jane

Jane 3 contains a number of features not present in Jane 2 such as failure to diverge events and time zone support. In addition, the randomization method used in statistics mode did not work correctly in Jane 2 and has been fixed in Jane 3. For this reason, it is recommended that you avoid using the statistics mode in Jane 2. Jane 3 also tends to be faster on larger trees (50 tips or more) while Jane 2 is faster for smaller trees. Both versions are available for download below.

Jane is easy to download and install. Choose the version for your particular platform or the platform independent version that works on all platforms.

When you click on a version below, most browsers will save the download in your "Downloads" directory/folder. If you wish to download it to another directory, most Windows browsers will allow you to right click and choose "Save as". On a Macintosh, hold down the control key and click.
